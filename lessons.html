LESSONS
My goal as a teacher is to provide a clear and organized path to improve one's skills. Time is precious and we should use it wisely!

Online learning is grate way to study nowadays . It provide the platform to study with musicians that normally you wouldn’t be able to reach. Whether you are across the country or across the globe, as long as you have a functioning internet connection and a webcam great guitar lessons can be yours! I have over five years of experience in distance-learning teaching, having students from all over the world.

  • • More flexibility to your own schedule.
  • • Either if you are looking for continuous lessons, or a master class format.
  • • Familiar with most of the classical guitar methods.
  • • Experienced with amateur students and people seeking a professional career as well.
  • • Genres of expertise are classical and Latin American music.
  • • The topics approached can be either musical interpretation, technical skills, music reading, music theory, performance coach or learning how to organize your practice.
